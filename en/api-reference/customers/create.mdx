---
title: "Create customer"
description: "Create a new customer"
openapi: "/api-reference/openapi.json POST /v1/customers"
---

Creates a new customer associated with your merchant account. Customers can be linked to transactions for better reporting and payment pre-filling.

## Parameters

<ParamField body="name" type="string" required>
  Customer's full name.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="email" type="string" required>
  Customer's email address.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="document" type="string">
  Customer's CPF or CNPJ (digits only, no punctuation).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="documentType" type="string">
  Document type: `CPF` (individual) or `CNPJ` (company).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="phone" type="string">
  Customer's phone number in international format. Example: `+5511999999999`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="externalId" type="string">
  Your internal ID for this customer. Must be unique per merchant.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="metadata" type="string">
  JSON string with custom data you want to store. Maximum 1000 characters.
</ParamField>
